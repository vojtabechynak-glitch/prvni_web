Minecraft's best tips + tricks

Mace

You can repair mace with mending, but even with breezerods on anvil.

PvP

Jump and hit while falling to deal critical damage.

Survival

You can create infinite lava using dripstone and a cauldron.

Mining

Always mine with a pickaxe on Y-level -59 for the best diamonds and ancient debris on Y-level -15.

Enchanting

Use bookshelves around your enchanting table to increase enchantment levels up to 30.

Building

Use different wood types and stairs to add depth and detail to your structures.
