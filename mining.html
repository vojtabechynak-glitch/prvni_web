Mining Tips

💎 Best Y-Level for Diamonds

Since the 1.18 cave update, diamonds generate most frequently at Y-level -58 to -59. Mine in a branch mining pattern: dig a main tunnel, then create branches every 3 blocks to the sides. This covers the most ground while using the fewest pickaxe swings, maximising your diamonds per hour.

🪓 Essential Enchantments for Mining

Efficiency V dramatically speeds up mining — essential for deep-level mining sessions. Unbreaking III triples your pickaxe's lifespan, meaning you spend far less time repairing. Fortune III can multiply diamond drops (up to 4 diamonds per ore), effectively tripling your haul. Silk Touch is useful for collecting ore blocks themselves to smelt later.

🔦 Torch Placement Strategy

When branch mining, always place torches on the right side of your tunnels. This creates a simple navigation rule: when returning to the surface, keep the torches on your left. You'll never get lost in deep cave systems again. Use redstone torches to mark dead ends or tunnels you've already explored.

🏚️ Ancient Debris & Netherite

Ancient Debris generates most frequently at Y-level 15 in the Nether. It is completely blast-resistant, so use beds or TNT explosions to quickly uncover large areas (beds explode in the Nether). You need 4 pieces of Ancient Debris to smelt into 4 Netherite Scraps, combined with 4 Gold Ingots, to craft a single Netherite Ingot.

🗺️ Protect Your Inventory

Before any deep mining session, always bring extra food, multiple torches, a water bucket, and a spare pickaxe. Consider leaving valuable items at your base — losing your Fortune III pickaxe to lava is devastating. Use an Ender Chest to securely store rare finds while mining so you never lose them to a death.

💡 Pro Tip — Ore Distribution at a Glance

🪨 Coal: Y 95 (surface) and Y 136 (mountains)
🔩 Iron: Y 16 and Y 232 (mountains)
🥇 Gold: Y -16 (deepslate), also very common in Badlands biomes
💎 Diamonds: Y -58 to -59 (peak frequency)
🔴 Redstone: Y -58 to -59 alongside diamonds
💠 Ancient Debris: Y 15 in the Nether
